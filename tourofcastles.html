Title: Tour of Castles
Programs used: Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, ArcMap
Description: This project was created for Grography 572: Graphic Design in Cartography.The proposed bicycle tour route takes place in Germany and visits castles along the way. The shaded relief was created from scratch, and I utilized creative labeling and visuals to highlight the bicycle route.
